Quantifier elimination QE decides truth of formulas in real arithmetic.

QE may potentially take a long time. Reducing the number of variables that occur in formulas helps. Use abbrv to simplify formulas together with hideL and hideR to hide irrelevant assumptions and conclusions prior to QE.

See also

  • Inspect → Find assumptions to get hints on how to strengthen the assumptions to make the sequent provable.
